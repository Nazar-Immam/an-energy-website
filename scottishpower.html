SECTION 01 — IDEA

One platform, four engineered moves

  • Advanced epoxy-composite blade skin in circular segmented sections
  • Demountable suction-bucket jacket for lower install and removal burden
  • Swap-ready nacelle for fast offshore replacement of critical units
  • AN.Energy PIeNN layer for predictive-preventive maintenance
Modular
Lower OPEX
Cleaner EoL

"If a part is expensive to access offshore, it must be modular. If it reaches end-of-life, it must be removable."

SECTION 02 — CONSTRUCTION

Port-built circular blade architecture

  • Manufacture compact blade segments near port using an advanced epoxy-composite shell
  • Assemble the blade ring at port instead of moving one oversized monolithic blade
  • Install on a suction-bucket jacket to shrink offshore lift time and noise
  • Lower vessel days means lower CAPEX and lower installation carbon
Smaller loads
Fewer lifts
Lower carbon

"The cost win is logistical: shorter weather-window exposure, less transport pain, and less reliance on premium offshore vessels."

SECTION 03 — OPERATION

AN.Energy turns a Normal turbine into a Forecastable Asset

  • PIeNN ingests SCADA, vibration, strain, weather and electrical signatures
  • Flag blade erosion, converter stress, drivetrain anomalies and foundation health are predicted early
  • Using swap-ready nacelle modules to convert long repairs duration into short replacement visits
  • Result: higher availability, fewer emergency interventions, lower lifetime OPEX
Predict
Act
Prevent

"Predict offshore failures earlier, then repair onshore when possible. Expensive heroics are replaced by planned swaps."

SECTION 04 — DECOMMISSIONING

Recover modules first, recycle what cannot be reused

  • Remove the turbine in modules instead of destructive cut-and-scrap campaigns
  • Reuse recovered steel and support-structure materials where site rules allow
  • Repurpose intact blade segments into coastal panels, walkways or secondary composite structures
  • Send damaged composite sections to grinding or chemical recovery routes
Reuse first
Recover second
Waste last

"The blade should not become landfill by default. Design it so intact sections can live a second useful life."
